# 📊 Financial Statement Automation Utility

> Excel/VBA tool integrated with Tally ERP that auto-generates client-ready 
> financial statements — deployed firm-wide to 50+ professionals.

## Overview
Built during my CA articleship at Rathi Rathi & Co. to eliminate the manual 
effort of preparing financial statements from scratch each engagement.

**Before:** 1–2 days per client (small), 2–3 days (larger clients)  
**After:** Under 1 day for most clients — a 50–60% reduction in prep time

## How It Works
1. Connects to the client's **Tally ERP** and extracts the Trial Balance
2. User maps ledgers into groups and sub-groups (one-time setup per client)
3. Tool automatically generates:
   - Balance Sheet, P&L, Cash Flow Statement
   - All applicable Notes to Accounts
   - Final formatting (hides blank rows, applies styles)
   - PDF export ready for review

## Tech Stack
- Microsoft Excel (Advanced)
- VBA (Macros)
- Power Query
- Tally ERP integration

## Impact
- Adopted firm-wide by **50+ professionals** across financial reporting and audit functions
- Reviewed and endorsed by the firm's Financial Reporting and Audit heads
- Covers all AS-compliant clients of the firm
